1 PHP Backuper {#mainpage}
2 ===
3 This is a framework which will help you making (incremental) backups of your site.
4 The framework is written in pure php an can be used on free hostings with supported PHP version ( 5.4+) and was designed as extensible.
5 Feel free to fork and modify it.

73 Backupers
74 ---
75 Backupers are responsible for extracting data, packing them to archive and mantaining index.
76 Each backuper must implement IBackuper interface.
77 
78 
79 Uploaders
80 ---
81 Uploaders are responsible for upload resulting archive to different services such as DropBox, SugarSync, Google Drive, Yandex.Disk, etc.
82 Each uploader must implement IUploader interface.
